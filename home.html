Coach Derek

Coach Derek aka Derek Locklear strives to make each day better for the youth. His company works with children from the West side to South Los Angeles.

Coach emphasizes to "coach to teach and not to win." He works year round to help keep the youth active, healthy and full of life through his many programs and camps that are for boys and girls alike.

Camp

Coach Derek Camp is for children ages 3 - 12 years old. There are both half day and full day camps available. If your child loves to play and interact with other kids, then this is the perfect camp for them!

Drop-ins are welcome anytime during open hours!
